Savage Worlds Funnels

  • "Funnel" as in the Dungeon Crawl Classics (DCC) and old school RPG concept. Start with many level-0 adventurers. One survivor per player advances to level 1.
  • Refresh for another batch of characters.
  • Some quick rules:
    • Characters are extras.
    • All trait rolls are d4.
    • Profession-relevant trait rolls are made at d6.
    • Do not roll a wild die.
    • Start with 0 bennies.
    • Start with 2 extras on the front lines. The rest mill about uselessly.
    • If an extra dies, replace with another of your extras.
    • If all your extras die, take control of another player's extra. Their choice.
  • Based on the Savage Worlds Deluxe USS Kaine adventure, this should result in about a 12.5% survival rate for the final fight assuming no bennies are spent. If you want a 30% survival rate, grant either 1 benny or 1 wound. If you want a 50% survival rate, grant both.
  • I suspect the actual survival rate will be much higher.
